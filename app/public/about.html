Technical Protocol

AI-Powered State Machine

AdPact replaces human intermediaries with decentralized consensus. Every collaboration moves through strict cryptographic states verified by independent validator nodes.

sync_alt State Machine Flow

Every sponsorship deal transitions through a secure sequence of states to enforce compliance and eliminate chargebacks:

  • check_circle
    Open for Applications: Creators submit handles & proposal text.
  • check_circle
    Escrow Deposited: Advertiser approves the applicant, locking the budget in the contract.
  • check_circle
    Draft Approved: Text drafts are aligned and signed off by the advertiser.
  • check_circle
    AI Consensus Check: Nodes fetch the live tweet and run NLP-checks in parallel.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the AdPact ecosystem.

business_center

For Advertisers

How is my budget protected? expand_more

Your funds are held in a transparent smart contract. They cannot be accessed by the creator until the AI nodes verify that the post meets your specific criteria.

What if the post is deleted early? expand_more

Campaigns specify a mandatory "Live Duration." If our verification nodes detect the post has been removed before this window closes, the payout is halted and the dispute mechanism is triggered.

How accurate is the AI verification? expand_more

Our AI uses a multi-layered verification system with over 99.8% accuracy, cross-referencing visual elements, metadata, and platform APIs simultaneously.

person_check

For Creators

When do I get paid? expand_more

Immediately. The smart contract executes the payment the second the verification nodes confirm your post meets the criteria. No manual approval required from the brand.

What platforms are supported? expand_more

Currently, we support Instagram (Posts, Reels, Stories), TikTok, X (Twitter), and YouTube. We are expanding to LinkedIn and Twitch in late 2024.

Is my payment guaranteed? expand_more

Yes. Because brands must fund the escrow contract *before* you start working, the money is already there. If you fulfill the requirements, the code guarantees your payout.

How do I get testnet tokens (GEN)? expand_more

To interact with AdPact on the testnet, you need test GEN tokens. You can request them instantly for free from our network faucets:

• For Bradbury Testnet: Visit the Bradbury Faucet
• For StudioNet: Claim them directly on the GenLayer Studio Contracts page
